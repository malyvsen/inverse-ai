Inverse AI

Bachelor thesis: transferring neural networks from computers to human brains.

Instructions

I turned 256 movie descriptions into pictures with AI. The pictures have nothing in common with the movies at first glance, but the AI did follow some rules while creating them - let's see if you can figure it out! I myself don't actually know these rules, they were made up by the AI.

Don't worry if this seems obscure at first, you'll get feedback after every answer - and hopefully will be able to learn.
